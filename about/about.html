Alexenferman.com is blog focused on device modding to get the most out of it, by rooting Android phones, installing custom or alternative operating systems and aftermarket mods.
The original Alexenferman website was founded in March 2020 by Alex T. originally hosted on Google Sites. We never thought this was someting serious, however we have migrated in September 2020 to a full website made in HTML and CSS. With around 200 visitors per day, and around 9000 users per months we are more than happy to have visitors on our site who enjoy our content. Although there is not too much content, we are planning to release a lot of content and many other things as well soon.
